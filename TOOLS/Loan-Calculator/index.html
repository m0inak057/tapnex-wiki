Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is this EMI calculator accurate?

A: Yes, this calculator uses the standard mathematical formula for calculating Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs). It is highly accurate for fixed-rate loans.

Q: What is an EMI?

A: EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It's the fixed amount you pay to your lender every month. This payment includes both a portion of the original loan (principal) and the interest charged on it.

Q: Is this tool really free?

A: Yes, our loan EMI calculator is completely free to use. All calculations happen in your browser, so your financial data is 100% private.
