ðŸŽ¨ Color Harmony Types

Our generator creates palettes using proven color theory principles:

  • Analogous: Colors that are adjacent on the color wheel, creating harmonious and cohesive palettes
  • Complementary: Colors opposite each other on the wheel for bold, high-contrast designs
  • Triadic: Three colors evenly spaced around the wheel for vibrant, balanced palettes
  • Monochromatic: Variations of a single hue with different saturations and lightness

ðŸ’¡ Color Theory Tips

  • 60-30-10 Rule: Use dominant (60%), secondary (30%), and accent (10%) colors
  • Contrast is Key: Ensure sufficient contrast for readability and accessibility
  • Test Before Use: Preview colors on different backgrounds and contexts
  • Consider Psychology: Colors evoke emotions - blue for trust, red for energy, green for growth
  • Accessibility: Check WCAG contrast ratios for text and background combinations
  • Cultural Context: Colors have different meanings across cultures
