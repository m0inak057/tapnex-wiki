How to Use the GST Calculator

To Add GST (Exclusive):

  1. Enter the original amount (before tax) in the "Amount" field.
  2. Enter the GST rate (e.g., 5, 12, 18).
  3. Click the "Add GST" button.
  4. The tool will show you the GST amount and the new total.

To Remove GST (Inclusive):

  1. Enter the total amount (including tax) in the "Amount" field.
  2. Enter the GST rate that was applied (e.g., 18).
  3. Click the "Remove GST" button.
  4. The tool will show you the original base amount and the GST amount.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is this calculator for GST only?

A: This calculator uses a standard percentage formula. You can use it to calculate any value-added tax (VAT) or sales tax, not just GST.

Q: Is this tool free and secure?

A: Yes, it is 100% free. All calculations are done in your browser, so your financial data is completely private.
