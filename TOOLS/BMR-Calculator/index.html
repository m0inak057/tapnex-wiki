What is BMR?

Your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) is the number of calories your body needs to perform basic, life-sustaining functions like breathing, circulation, and cell production while you are at rest. This calculator uses the popular Mifflin-St Jeor equation to estimate your BMR.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is this different from a Daily Calorie Calculator?

A: Yes. Your BMR is just your calories at rest. A Daily Calorie Calculator (or TDEE calculator) will take your BMR and add calories based on your daily activity level.

Q: Is this tool free and secure?

A: Yes, it is 100% free. All calculations are done in your browser, so your health data is completely private.
