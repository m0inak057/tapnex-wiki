How to Use the Age Calculator

  1. Select your Date of Birth using the date picker.
  2. The "Calculate Age as of" date defaults to today, but you can change it if needed.
  3. Click "Calculate Age".
  4. Your age will be displayed in years, months, and days.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Is this calculator accurate?

A: Yes, this calculator accounts for leap years and the varying number of days in each month to give you an accurate age calculation.

Q: Is this tool free and secure?

A: Yes, it's 100% free. All calculations happen in your browser, keeping your data private.
