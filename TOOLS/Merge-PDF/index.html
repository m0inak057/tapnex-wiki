How to Merge PDF Files for Free

  1. Upload Files: Click the "Choose Files" button or drag and drop your PDF files into the upload area.
  2. Order Files: The files will appear in a list below. Drag and drop the files to set the correct order for merging.
  3. Merge PDFs: Once your files are in order, click the "Merge PDFs" button.
  4. Download: Your merged PDF will be created in seconds. Click the "Download" button to save it.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this PDF merger secure?

Yes! This tool is 100% secure. All processing is done in your browser using JavaScript. Your files are never uploaded to a server, so your data remains private.

Is this tool really free?

Yes, our PDF merger is completely free to use.

How many PDF files can I combine at once?

You can upload and merge multiple files at once. The limit is based on your browser's memory, but it can handle most common tasks.

Will my merged PDF lose quality?

No, the tool combines your PDFs without re-compressing them, ensuring the original quality is maintained.
