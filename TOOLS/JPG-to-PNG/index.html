Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this JPG to PNG converter secure?

Yes! This tool is 100% secure. All processing is done in your browser using JavaScript and the HTML Canvas. Your image file is never uploaded to a server, so your data remains private.

Why convert from JPG to PNG?

People convert JPG to PNG to get a "lossless" image format, which means it doesn't lose quality when saved. It's also the first step if you later want to edit the image and add a transparent background.

Will my image quality be as good?

Yes. PNG is a "lossless" format, which means it will perfectly preserve all the detail and quality from your original JPG file.
