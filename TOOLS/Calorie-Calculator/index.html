How to Use the Daily Calorie Calculator

  1. Enter your age, gender, height (cm), and weight (kg).
  2. Select your typical daily activity level from the dropdown.
  3. Click "Calculate Calories".
  4. The results show your estimated daily calorie needs for maintaining your current weight, as well as for mild or moderate weight loss or gain.

What is TDEE?

TDEE stands for Total Daily Energy Expenditure. It's the total number of calories your body burns in a day, including your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR) and the calories burned through physical activity. This calculator estimates your TDEE based on the Mifflin-St Jeor BMR formula and standard activity multipliers.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: Are these calorie numbers exact?

A: No, these are estimates. Individual calorie needs can vary based on factors like body composition, genetics, and health conditions. Use this as a starting point.

Q: Is this tool free and secure?

A: Yes, it's 100% free. All calculations happen in your browser, keeping your health data private.
