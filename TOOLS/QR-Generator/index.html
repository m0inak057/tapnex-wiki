‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

What is a QR code?

A QR (Quick Response) code is a two-dimensional barcode that can store various types of information such as URLs, text, contact information, WiFi credentials, and more. QR codes can be scanned using smartphone cameras or dedicated QR code readers, making them perfect for quick information sharing.

How do I use the generated QR code?

After generating your QR code, simply download it as a PNG image. You can then print it on business cards, flyers, posters, or product packaging. You can also use it digitally on websites, social media, or email signatures. Anyone with a smartphone can scan it to access your content instantly.

Can I customize the size of my QR code?

Yes! Our QR code generator allows you to create QR codes in different sizes to suit your needs. Whether you need a small code for a business card or a large one for a billboard, you can adjust the size before downloading. The code remains scannable at any size as long as it's clear and properly printed.

Are the QR codes permanent?

The QR codes generated by our tool are static, meaning the content is permanently encoded into the code itself. Once generated, the QR code will always point to the same URL or contain the same text. This makes them reliable for long-term use on printed materials. However, if the URL destination changes or goes offline, the QR code will no longer work.

What file format are the QR codes?

Our generator creates QR codes in PNG format, which is a high-quality, lossless image format. PNG files work great for both digital and print use, maintaining clarity at any size. The transparent background makes it easy to overlay on different colored backgrounds.
