üí° Percentage Calculation Formulas

  • Find X% of Y: Formula = (X √∑ 100) √ó Y. Example: 20% of 150 = (20 √∑ 100) √ó 150 = 30
  • X is what % of Y: Formula = (X √∑ Y) √ó 100. Example: 30 is what % of 150? = (30 √∑ 150) √ó 100 = 20%
  • Percentage Change: Formula = ((New - Old) √∑ Old) √ó 100. Example: From 100 to 120 = ((120 - 100) √∑ 100) √ó 100 = +20%
  • Percentage Increase: Add percentage to original. Example: 100 + 20% = 100 + (20% of 100) = 120
  • Percentage Decrease: Subtract percentage from original. Example: 100 - 20% = 100 - (20% of 100) = 80
