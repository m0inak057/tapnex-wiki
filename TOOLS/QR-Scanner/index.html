QR Code Scanner

Scan QR codes using your device's camera. No app needed.

How to Scan a QR Code Online for Free

  1. Start Scan: Click the "Start Camera Scan" button.
  2. Grant Permission: Your browser will ask for permission to use your camera. You must click "Allow".
  3. Point Camera: A video feed will appear. Point your phone or laptop camera at the QR code.
  4. Get Result: The tool will automatically scan the code and display the text, link, or data in the "Result" box.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this QR Code Scanner secure?

Yes! This tool is 100% secure. Your camera feed is processed entirely within your browser and is never recorded or uploaded to our servers.

Do I need to download an app?

No. This tool works in any modern web browser (like Chrome, Safari, or Firefox) on your computer or smartphone.

My camera isn't working.

Please ensure you clicked "Allow" when the browser asked for camera permission. Also, check that no other app (like Zoom or Teams) is currently using your camera.
