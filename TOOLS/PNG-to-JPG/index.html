Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Is this PNG to JPG converter secure?

Yes! This tool is 100% secure. All processing is done in your browser using JavaScript and the HTML Canvas. Your image file is never uploaded to a server, so your data remains private.

What happens to the transparency in my PNG?

The JPG format does not support transparency. Our tool allows you to pick a background color (like white) that will fill in the transparent areas of your original image.

Will I lose image quality?

JPG is a "lossy" format, so there is always some compression. However, our tool lets you control the quality with a slider. A setting of 0.9 or 1.0 will give you a very high-quality result.

Is this tool really free?

Yes, our PNG to JPG converter is completely free to use.
