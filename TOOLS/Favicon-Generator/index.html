❓ Frequently Asked Questions About Favicons

  • Q: What is a favicon?
    A: A favicon is the small icon that appears in browser tabs, bookmarks, and next to your site name in search results.
  • Q: Why do I need multiple favicon sizes?
    A: Different devices and platforms require different sizes for optimal display quality and user experience.
  • Q: How do I add favicons to my website?
    A: Upload the PNG files to your website root and add the provided HTML link tags to your page's <head> section.
  • Q: Can I use a logo as my favicon?
    A: Yes! Logos make excellent favicons, but ensure they're simple enough to be recognizable at 16×16 pixels.
  • Q: Are favicons important for SEO?
    A: Yes! Favicons improve brand recognition in search results and can increase click-through rates.
  • Q: What file format should I upload?
    A: PNG or JPEG. Our tool converts them to optimized PNG favicons automatically.
