‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Is this password generator secure?

Yes! Our password generator uses cryptographically secure random number generation (crypto.getRandomValues()) to create truly random passwords. All processing happens entirely in your browser, meaning passwords are never sent to our servers or stored anywhere. Your generated passwords are completely private and secure.

What makes a strong password?

A strong password should be at least 12-16 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. It should not contain dictionary words, personal information, or predictable patterns. Our generator creates passwords that meet all these criteria, making them highly resistant to brute-force attacks.

Can I customize the password options?

Absolutely! You have full control over your password generation. You can adjust the length (8-32 characters) and choose which character types to include: uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. This flexibility allows you to meet specific password requirements for different websites or applications.

Are my generated passwords stored anywhere?

No, we never store, save, or transmit your generated passwords. Everything happens locally in your browser. Once you close the page or generate a new password, the previous password is gone forever unless you've saved it yourself. This ensures complete privacy and security for your password generation.
