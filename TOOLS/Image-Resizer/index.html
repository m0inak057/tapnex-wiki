  • Website Images: Resize to exact dimensions needed to improve page load speed and SEO
  • Quality: For best results, resize from larger to smaller rather than upscaling

❓ Common Questions About Image Resizing

  • Q: Will resizing reduce image quality?
    A: When downsizing (making smaller), quality is maintained. Upsizing may reduce quality.
  • Q: What's the best format for resized images?
    A: JPEG for photos, PNG for graphics with transparency, WebP for modern web use.
  • Q: Can I resize multiple images?
    A: Currently one at a time for precise control over each image's dimensions.
  • Q: How do I maintain image proportions?
    A: Keep the "Maintain Aspect Ratio" checkbox enabled when resizing.
  • Q: Is there a file size limit?
    A: No hard limit, but very large images may take longer to process in the browser.
