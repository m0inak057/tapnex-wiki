🎨 Ad Container Styling Demo - Jain Docs

Note: This is a demonstration of how ad containers will appear on your Jain Docs pages. The green boxes represent where actual AdSense ads will appear.

1. Top Ad Container

This appears at the top of pages. Class: .ad-container.ad-top

🌟 AdSense Top Ad
(Hover over me!)

2. Mid-Content Ad Container

These appear between content sections. Class: .ad-container.article-mid-ad

📰 AdSense In-Content Ad
(Most common placement)

3. Bottom Ad Container

Appears at the end of content. Class: .ad-container.ad-bottom

🎯 AdSense Bottom Ad
(End of page)

4. Sidebar Ad Container (Sticky)

Sidebar ads that stick while scrolling. Class: .ad-container.ad-sidebar

Main content goes here. The sidebar ad stays visible while you scroll down the page.

This is especially useful for long-form content pages.

📌 Sticky Sidebar Ad
(Stays visible)
